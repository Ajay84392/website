Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Hospitals provide post-treatment care through follow-up appointments, medication management, cardiac rehabilitation programs, support groups, and lifestyle counselling.

To plan cardiac treatment in India, begin by consulting a cardiologist for diagnosis and treatment recommendations. Research reputable hospitals with experienced cardiac specialists and assess insurance coverage for procedures and potential costs. Compare treatment options and costs across different facilities and arrange travel and accommodation if necessary.

Factors that affect the treatment cost at top cardiac surgery hospitals in New Delhi are the type of cardiac procedure, the expertise of the cardiac surgeon, diagnostic tests performed, hospital fees, and the duration of the hospital stay. The use of advanced technology, such as minimally invasive procedures or specialised post-operative care and rehabilitation, also influences the overall cost.

The best cardiac surgery hospitals in New Delhi offer various diagnostic tests and services like CT scans, MRI scans, ECG, EKG, stress test, Holter monitoring, etc. These tests are done to accurately detect and plan treatments for congenital heart defects, CAD, irregular heart beat, and other heart conditions.

When you have to select the best cardiac surgery hospitals in New Delhi, you should consider factors like the hospital’s accreditations, such as JCI and NABH, the experience of cardiologists and cardiac surgeons, and the availability of advanced treatment facilities. Along with this, you should verify the success rate of minimally invasive heart surgeries and provide patient reviews.

A cardiac ICU is critical for patients undergoing complex cardiac procedures or experiencing heart failure. It provides constant monitoring, specialised care, and immediate intervention if complications arise.

Cardiac rehabilitation is essential for recovery and improving heart health. It involves exercise, education, and lifestyle modifications under medical supervision.

The top cardiac surgery hospitals in New Delhi provide a wide range of advanced treatments, such as: Minimally invasive cardiac procedures Robotic cardiac surgery Cardiac resynchronisation therapy (CRT) Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO)

The hospital stay and recovery period at the best cardiac surgery hospitals in New Delhi vary based on the type of procedure and the patient’s overall health. For angioplasty and stent placement, the patient should expect around 2-3 days in the hospital whereas recovery may take around 4-6 weeks. In very complex cases like a heart transplant, the patient usually requires to spend time in the hospital for 2-4 weeks. Even after that, though, recovery might take a couple of months.
